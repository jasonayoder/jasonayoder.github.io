## Reading & Podcasts

I aim to read books spanning personal productivity, evolution and development, theory of mind and consciousness, leadership, and science fiction. Some favorites:

- *What is Intelligence?* by Blaise Aguera y Arcas
- *Antifragile* by Nassim Nicholas Taleb
- *Sapiens* by Yuval Noah Harari
- *I Am a Strange Loop* by Douglas Hofstadter
- *How to Change Your Mind* by Michael Pollan
- *A Thousand Brains* by Jeff Hawkins
- *Range* by David Epstein
- *Drive* by Daniel Pink
- *Diaspora* and *Permutation City* by Greg Egan

Podcasts I follow: Lex Fridman, Brain-Inspired, and Optimal Living Daily.
