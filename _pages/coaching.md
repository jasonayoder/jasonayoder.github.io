Ultimate frisbee has been a constant in my life since college, and coaching has become one of my most meaningful leadership outlets.

## Rose-Hulman Ultimate Frisbee

I've served as the club coach for Rose-Hulman's Ultimate Frisbee team since November 2017, investing over 600 hours across six years. I develop offensive and defensive systems and plays, and I work closely with team captains on strategy and recruiting.

I nominated team captain Chris Miller for Student Leader of the Quarter---and he won (Winter 2019--20).

## Indianapolis Alleycats

In 2015, I was team captain of the Indianapolis Alleycats, a semi-professional Ultimate team in the American Ultimate Disc League.

## Indiana University

I coached the IU Men's Ultimate Frisbee team from January 2010 to May 2014 during my graduate studies.

## Community Ultimate

During the summers of 2022 and 2023, I organized an inclusive ultimate frisbee team in Bloomington, Indiana, open to players of all skill levels.

At Goshen College, I served as Ultimate Frisbee Club President (2007--2008).

## Other Leadership

- Social Chair, CS Graduate Student Association, Indiana University (2010--2011)
- Indoor Roller Hockey Club President, Goshen College (2005--2007)
- Project Manager, Project Management Class, Goshen College (2007)
- Volunteer Camp Counselor, Laurelville Mennonite Church Center, Mt. Pleasant, PA (July 2006)
