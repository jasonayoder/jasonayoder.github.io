Ultimate frisbee has been a constant in my life since college, and coaching has become one of my most meaningful leadership outlets.

## Rose-Hulman Ultimate Frisbee

I've served as the club coach for Rose-Hulman's Ultimate Frisbee team since November 2017. I develop offensive and defensive systems and plays, and I work closely with team captains on strategy, engagement, and recruiting.

## Indiana University

I coached the IU Men's Ultimate Frisbee team from January 2010 to May 2014 during my graduate studies.

## Community Ultimate

During the summers of 2022 and 2023, I organized an inclusive ultimate frisbee team (Los Heros) in Bloomington, Indiana, open to players of all skill levels.

At Goshen College, I served as Ultimate Frisbee Club President (2007--2008).
