# Research & Publications

## Research Interests
Neuromodulation, artificial neural networks, neuroevolution, artificial life, evolutionary computation, affective computing, cognitive development, philosophy of mind, minimally cognitive agents, evolvable hardware, embodied/embedded cognition, evolutionary development, origins of life, origins of learning, origins of consciousness

## Lab
**EASY Lab** (Evolutionary and Adaptive Systems at Yoder's Lab) - with Moodle-based onboarding site

## Active Research Projects

**1. Modeling Evolutionary Development (EvoDevo) (2019-2024)**
- Minimal models of development in tunably rugged NK fitness landscapes
- Publications: ALIFE 2022, ALIFE 2023, ALIFE 2024
- Students: Jacob Ashworth, Yujung Lee, Jackson Shen, Edward Kim, Zach Decker, Julian Fiorito, Alexa Renner, Emile Marois

**2. Evolvable Hardware (2018-2024)**
- Collaboration with Derek Whitley (IU). Evolved circuits using FPGAs
- Publications: ALIFE 2021, Artificial Life Journal 2022
- Project website: https://evolvablehardware.org/
- Students: Nicklas Carpenter, Allyn Loyd, Daniel Gaull, Logan Manthey (grew to 7 students)

**3. Biologically Plausible Reinforcement Learning with CTRNNs**
- Collaboration with Eduardo Izquierdo (IU). NSF Research Opportunity Award (~$24,000, Grant 1845322)
- Published: Frontiers in Computational Neuroscience 2022
- Students: Cooper Anderson, Adrian Wang (Cehong Wang)

**4. Developmental Artificial Neural Networks**
- Growing networks, brain-body co-development, activity dependence
- Students: Adrian Wang, Cullen LaKemper (GECCO 2022), Yingtong Zhang (ALIFE 2024 + Artificial Life journal)
- Senior Research: David Gottlieb, Julian Fiorito, Dylan Luttrell, Helen Wang (self-aware neural architecture)

**5. Quality Diversity for GANs**
- Student: Mingyang Cai - demonstrated robustness to mode collapse

**6. Education Research**
- Exam Wrappers: IEEE CSEE&T 2020 (with Steve Chenoweth) - analyzed ~1000 student exam scores
- Incentive Points System: Submitted to SIGCSE 2025 (with Aaron Wilkin, Mitchel Daniel)
